About Insurtech Insights

Insurtech Insights is an independent publication covering the intersection of artificial intelligence and insurance technology.

As the insurance industry navigates its most significant technological transformation in decades, we provide data-driven analysis, practical implementation guides, and unbiased comparisons of AI tools and platforms reshaping the sector.

What We Cover

Our coverage spans six core domains: AI in claims processing, underwriting automation, fraud detection and prevention, embedded insurance distribution, policy administration and customer experience, and the enterprise decision intelligence driving AI adoption across the industry.

How We Operate

Our content is produced using advanced AI language models trained on publicly available insurance industry knowledge, curated and reviewed to maintain accuracy and editorial standards. We are transparent about our AI-assisted workflow and continuously improve our output through feedback and iteration.
